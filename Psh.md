1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?
* They come in 3 types. Visual, Verbal and Physical. 
* Visual involves inappropriate drawings, posters, emails/texts of sexual nature.
* Verbal involves comments of someone's body, clothes or sexual jokes that are unwelcome. Repeatedly asking someone out could also
  be termed as sexual harassment.
* Physical involves sexual assault or inappropriate body contact like touching, kissing, blocking etc.
2. What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?
* I will inform the concerned authorities as soon as possible.
